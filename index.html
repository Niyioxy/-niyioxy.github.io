Overview

Mo'jere is a revolutionary mobile application tailored for FMCG wholesalers in Nigeria. Our app streamlines inventory management, sales tracking, and customer engagement with features like text-to-speech ordering, barcode scanning, and digital invoicing. Designed for simplicity and scalability, Mo'jere empowers users to manage their businesses efficiently—whether they operate a single store or multiple outlets.

Market Opportunity

Nigeria’s FMCG market is vast, with over 166,000 wholesalers actively engaged in distribution. Our market analysis indicates:

  • TAM: 1,000,000 wholesalers | ₦60B revenue potential annually.
  • SAM: 500,000 wholesalers | ₦30B revenue potential annually.
  • SOM: 166,000 wholesalers | ₦9.96B revenue potential annually.

This significant opportunity positions Mo'jere as a key player in digitizing the traditional wholesale market.

Product Details

Core Features

  • Real-time inventory management across multiple stores.
  • Comprehensive sales tracking and performance analytics.
  • Innovative text-to-speech (TTS) ordering for local language support.
  • Digital invoicing delivered via SMS/WhatsApp.
  • Customer database with buying trend insights.

Technology Stack

Our tech stack leverages React Native for cross-platform development, Node.js and Express for backend logic, PostgreSQL and Firebase for real-time databases, and AI-powered tools (Google Cloud Speech-to-Text) for TTS functionality. This robust architecture ensures scalability, security, and efficiency.
